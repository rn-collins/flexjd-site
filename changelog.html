What's New

A running log of real updates to this site

No filler entries. Every line below reflects an actual change that shipped — new opportunity listings, fixed bugs, new tools. If you're wondering whether something changed recently, check here before assuming.

—

Update Log

Jul 22, 2026
Second-pass Opportunity Tracker audit — link destinations, not just link validity. Re-checked every Remote & Part-Time Clerkships listing against a stricter bar: does the destination page show an actual current, specific position to apply to, not just a resolving, topically-relevant page. Removed 7 more entries whose links loaded fine but led to generic careers pages with no real listing (Immigration Equality, VoteRiders, Center for Biological Diversity, Human Rights First, Center for Reproductive Rights, Center for Appellate Litigation, and Animal Legal Defense Fund). Combined with the earlier pass, that's 12 entries removed from 383. Re-checked FIRE, Institute for Justice, NY Attorney General, and SELC against the same standard — all four held up, since their listings honestly describe ongoing rolling programs rather than claiming a single dead-end posting. Sourced and verified three solid replacements: Liberty Justice Center's Fall 2026 remote internship ($20/hr, apply by Aug 15), Amica Center for Immigrant Rights' fully remote Fall 2026 Immigration Impact Lab internship (apply by Jul 31), and Pennsylvania Institutional Law Project's remote-eligible Fall 2026 semester externship — bringing the tracker to 372. Continued the audit through more of the list: NCLR, NCLC, GLAD Law, and Privacy Rights Clearinghouse all held up (real, current, specific programs), but Farmers Insurance's linked job posting had expired with no Law Clerk role among its current openings, and HRC's internship program turned out to be closed to applications for every 2026 term. Removed both, landing at 370 verified opportunities. Also fixed a display bug where long URLs in the Link column wrapped character-by-character, and trimmed overlong Notes cells to their key fact. Finished the full pass through every remaining Remote & Part-Time Clerkships row: Allstate, Shute Mihaly & Weinberger, ACLU of Missouri, Environmental Integrity Project, EEOC, Illinois Attorney General, NIPNLG, Massachusetts AG's Office, LWV, AFSA, The Lawyering Project, The Commons Law Center, ELC-PA, GAGV, PLAN, Cohen Weiss and Simon, Wikimedia Foundation, AWI, Animal Outlook, Juvenile Law Center, NDRC, NCUIH, FPF, Education Law Center, CAIDP, The Leadership Conference on Civil and Human Rights, Colorado Department of Law, and Center for Justice and Accountability all held up under the strict standard — real, current, specific programs, most on rolling admission. The one exception: CDC's Public Health Law Program page still described a 2025 cohort whose application window and appointment had already closed, with no current cycle open. Removed it, landing the tracker at a fully audited 369 verified opportunities — every single listing now checked against the "does it show a real, applyable position" bar.
Jul 16, 2026
Live search added to the Student Guide. The whole guide — FAQ entries, staff directory, course tables, org lists — is now searchable from one box; matching FAQ items auto-expand and empty sections hide themselves. Also fixed a layout bug left over from an earlier edit where part of Section 1 (Class-Year Pages, Disability Access Services) had been rendering outside its intended container.
Jul 16, 2026
Live search added to the Opportunity Tracker. All 380+ listings across all ten categories are now searchable by name, sponsor, topic, deadline, or eligibility from a single search bar — no more scrolling ten separate tables.
Jul 14–16, 2026
Remote & Part-Time Student Clerkships grew from 17 to 56 verified listings through 20+ rounds of individually verified research — including additions from the Center for Justice and Accountability, the Institute for Justice, Colorado's Department of Law, the Leadership Conference on Civil and Human Rights, CAIDP, and the Education Law Center, among others. Every listing is checked against its own posting page before it goes live; unconfirmed remote status is flagged rather than hidden.
Jul 4, 2026
Sitewide audit pass: added canonical/og:url tags on every page, published sitemap.xml and robots.txt, added aria-hidden to decorative SVGs, added the Opportunity Tracker's reliability disclaimer and the Newsletter's federal-loan disclaimer, and restructured the Student Guide's FAQ so mislabeled items landed in the right section.
Jul 3, 2026
Opportunity Tracker launched with its first Remote & Part-Time Legal Work section — 15 verified platforms and 9 individual employer openings to start.
Jul 3, 2026
Site launched: homepage, Student Guide, Newsletter, Opportunity Tracker, and all seven monthly Advocacy Campaign pages.

This log covers site-content and feature changes only, not every minor copy edit. If something looks off or missing, email the SBA Chair — see the footer.

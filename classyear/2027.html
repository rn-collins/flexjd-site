FlexJD Class of 2027

Cohort-specific co-op requirements, key contacts, and accommodations info. Expected graduation: June 2027.

01

Co-op & Experiential Requirement

The co-op requirement for the Class of 2027 follows the one co-op plus one experiential-semester structure ASA currently publishes.

RequirementDetail
Co-opOne co-op — a field placement with concurrent enrollment in a reflections seminar, totaling 8 credits.
Second practical experienceOne other semester-long practical experience: a co-op, field placement, or approved experiential project (professor-run team-based policy project, practically-focused research assistant position, University Experiential Network project, or Community-Engaged Teaching and Research project). Minimum 275 hours during the non-academic semester.
Total work-experience hours1,000 hours of other relevant work experience across the four-year program. Students with 3+ years of professional experience prior to matriculation may request a waiver from the Associate Dean for Experiential Education.

Source: ASA — FlexJD Co-op Requirements. Verify against your own cohort's handbook, since requirements can be grandfathered by matriculation year — ask ASA directly if anything here doesn't match what you were told at orientation.

02

Disability Access Services (DAS)

Northeastern renamed the Disability Resource Center to Disability Access Services (DAS) in July 2024 — DAS is the current name regardless of which term your handbook uses. Full guide to the accommodations process, including the co-op-specific note, is in the All-Purpose Guide.

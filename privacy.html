Privacy and Data-Handling Notice

This site is intended to provide public student resources. It should not be used to submit confidential, sensitive, or personally identifying information.

Information not requested

Do not submit grades, disability or medical information, financial-aid records, Social Security numbers, student identification numbers, immigration records, private application materials, private correspondence, or nonpublic contact lists.

Public information

The site may reproduce or link to information intentionally made public by Northeastern, employers, courts, nonprofits, government agencies, and other opportunity providers. Public availability does not remove the obligation to use only the minimum information necessary and to preserve source context.

Analytics and hosting

The site is hosted through Vercel. Hosting providers may process ordinary technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, request time, and requested page for security and service operation. Any analytics added later must be documented here before activation.

Corrections and removal

A person whose personal information appears on the site may request review, correction, or removal. Requests should identify the page and information at issue.

Repository rule

Private student records and secrets must never be committed to this public GitHub repository. See the repository SECURITY.md for reporting instructions.
